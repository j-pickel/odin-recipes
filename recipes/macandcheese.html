Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This is some really good homemade mac and cheese. It reminds me of the mac and cheese my mom used to make. Her mac and cheese was the perfect combination of cream and crunch. I have always been searching for the way to make it just like her. This may not be exactly like it, but it is my best version yet.

This recipe uses two different kinds of cheese. I like to use elbow macaroni, however, you can experiment with other kinds. I have used spiral and small shells before. The top is layered bith some bread crumbs to give a nice crunch mixed in. Milk and butter give the sauce a nice creamy texture all through.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shreddad sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1.5 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 0.25 cups butter
  • 2.5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 0.5 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch of paprika

Steps

  1. Cool macaroni according to package directions. Drain.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat. Stir in enough flour to make a roux. Add milk to roux slowly, stirring constantly. Stir in cheeses, and cook over low heat. Cook until cheese is melted and sauce is a little thick. Put macaroni in large casserole dish. Pour sauce over macaroni and stir will.
  3. Melt butter or margarine in a skiller over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and brown. Spread over macaroni and cheese to cover. Sprinkle with a little paprika,
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Serve
